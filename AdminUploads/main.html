Softwares. Some of the softwares I develope.

Web and computer desktop applications

I develope both tailor-made website and desktop applications that suit my client's needs. Upon request I come to your company,study your business structure,establish the system requirements and develope a responsive system that would aide in both marketing and facilitate operations in your company.

Android mobile application developement

I am an android mobile developer.I develope robust mobile applications with great appeal using material design.I often encourage my clients to have mobile applications working alongside their other management systems as android runs on fairly affordable devices plus the mobility is of much convenience.

Cyber Security

I am a cyber security agent certified by IBM with the IBM AppScan Cyber Security agent certificate.I have the skills to analyze your company's systems,check for vulnerabilities and give appropriate recommendations that would protect your company against attack by hackers or damage and loss of company resources due to such malicious attacks.

Reliable

I am a reliable software Engineer always exercising a high level of professionalism.Upon the initiation of a project,I work diligently to its completion and to the client's satisfaction.

Charges

My charges always vary depending on the complexity of your system and the resources that will be used in developing the system.There are levels of packages with regards to your financial capability but I always encourage to go for the best and we promise you will get the value for your money.

Maintenance

Maintenance will be considered as an after-sales service thus your system shall be maintained and supported by us all through its lifespan.There shall be no charges unless the maintenance warrants for use of extra resources.
